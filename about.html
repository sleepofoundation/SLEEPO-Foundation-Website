About Us

SLEEPO Foundation is a parent-founded nonprofit built on a simple belief — that the quiet moments before sleep are the most powerful moments in a child's day.

Our Mission

We create personalized bedtime audio stories designed to support confidence, positive values and cultural connection in children ages 2–12. Every story is thoughtfully created using information parents choose to share, including their child's interests, personality, and family values.

We believe bedtime is one of the few moments in the day when families can slow down, connect, and create lasting memories together.

SLEEPO Foundation is an IRS-recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in Redmond, Washington, USA.
