Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At SLEEPO Foundation, your family's privacy is fundamental to everything we do.
This page explains what we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over it.

Who We Are

SLEEPO Foundation is an IRS-recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in Redmond, Washington, USA. We create personalized bedtime audio stories designed to support confidence, resilience, values, and cultural connection.

This policy covers our websites at sleepofoundation.org, sleepo.org, and sleepokids.org. Our services are designed for parents and guardians of children ages 2–12.

What Information We Collect

About Your Child

  • Name, gender, and age
  • Interests, favorites, and personality traits you share with us
  • Social, personal growth, and developmental goals you wish to nurture

About You

  • Parent/guardian name and relationship to child
  • WhatsApp number and email address
  • Location (city, state, country)

Voice Features (if enabled)

Please note: If you choose to enable voice features, a recording of your voice will be processed by a third-party AI platform for story narration. You will be asked for explicit consent before this feature is activated. Voice recordings are retained only for as long as needed to provide the selected feature and may be deleted upon request.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create personalized audio stories for your child
  • To communicate with you about your child's experience
  • To improve our program over time

Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal information only to provide requested services, operate our nonprofit program, communicate with families, and meet legal obligations.

What We Never Do

  • Sell your information to anyone
  • Share your information with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Collect information directly from children

All information about children is submitted by parents or legal guardians. Children do not create accounts or submit information directly to SLEEPO.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party providers for:

  • Cloud hosting and storage
  • Authentication and account access
  • Database infrastructure
  • Audio generation and delivery
  • Analytics and website performance monitoring

We work with trusted service providers who process information to help us operate our services in accordance with their applicable terms and privacy practices. Some service providers may process data outside your state or country in accordance with applicable laws and their privacy practices.

We use privacy-focused analytics to understand website usage trends and do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information through analytics.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your family's information. All data transmitted through our website is encrypted using standard HTTPS/SSL protocols. Access to your data is restricted to authorized SLEEPO team members only. While we take reasonable measures to protect information, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and support program operations. You may request deletion at any time. Deletion requests are typically completed within 30 days unless legal or operational retention requirements apply.

Parental Rights Under COPPA

Because our program serves children under the age of 13, parents and legal guardians have full control over their child's data under the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

At any time, you have the right to:

  • Review the information we have collected about your child
  • Request deletion of that information
  • Refuse to allow us to collect or use any further information
🔍 Review your data 🗑️ Request deletion 🚫 Refuse further collection

Please be aware that if you request deletion of your child's profile data, they will no longer be able to receive personalized stories.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete any information we hold about your family by contacting us directly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent version.

Questions or Requests?

Reach out any time — we're happy to answer questions about your family's data or help you exercise your privacy rights.
